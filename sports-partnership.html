Sports Partnership

  • Home
  • Sports Partnership

At Ramagya we yield to increase the self- esteem and mental alertness of the students. We all are aware of the fact that sports are paramount in our lives and hence Ramagya has left no stone unturned to provide the best sports facilities to its students. Within a very short span of time, Ramagya has carved a niche for itself by giving equal weightage to all the sports. Also the team of coaches for all the sports at school, is unparalleled.
With the same view in mind, Ramagya has collaborated with towering academies and sports organisations. These partnerships enable the school to give a wider exposure to the glistening sports students as these enable the school to be a part of national and international competitions. The school also gets to cater several Games Programs, giving commodious opportunities to Ramagyans.


NBA Basketball School

Ramagya School has a new addition to its Sports armory and in collaboration with NBA BASKETBALL SCHOOL, it is the best Basketball academy in Noida. The Academy has two newly constructed international sized courts that will give an opportunity to its members to hone their skills as a basketball player. The basketball courts at Ramagya School are FIBA (Fédération Internationale de Basketball, commonly known as International Basketball Federation) approved. Tips from foreign coaches provide an impetus to our members and enthuse them to aspire and work with fervor for becoming national and consequently international players of repute. We at Ramagya School provide an opportunity to our members to play basketball in the arenas at par with world standards.

Sporty Beans

Ramagya’s collaboration with Sporty Beans is specifically for sports based play programme for preschool and primary school children between the ages of 2.5 & 8 years. The programme targets to develop confidence, concentration, social interaction, sportsmanship values and many such values in a caring and fun filled environment.Various activities encompassed at Sporty Beans are- Multi Sports Activity: This flagship program runs over the entire year, with two classes per week covering 9 ball sports – Soccer, Baseball, Basketball, Volleyball, Golf, Tennis, Cricket, Rugby and Hockey.

PADUKONE Sports Management

Discover the pinnacle of badminton excellence at Ramagya School, where passion meets precision and champions are forged. Under the esteemed guidance of ‘PADUKONE Sports Management’ led by PRAKASH PADUKONE, our academy offers an unrivaled environment for players of all ages and skill levels to thrive. With an unwavering commitment to excellence, we combine state-of-the-art facilities with expert coaching to cultivate the next generation of badminton stars. Led by highly qualified coaches, our comprehensive training programs encompass technical proficiency, physical conditioning, and mental resilience, ensuring that every player receives personalized attention tailored to their individual needs. From structured drills to competitive matches, our curriculum is designed to foster holistic development, empowering players to excel both on and off the court. From personalized attention to competitive opportunities, our holistic approach to badminton development ensures that every aspect of a player's game is honed to perfection.
Join us and embark on an extraordinary journey towards badminton mastery, where every smash, drop, and rally brings you closer to realizing your full potential.

Shooting Academy

The Academy aims at recognizing potential among young kids and adults and help them in accomplishing their goals by planning and managing trainees in a very methodical way.

CRIC Kingdom

Ramagya School aims to fulfill the dreams of young budding cricketers with boundless opportunities with the best cricket academy in noida. As part of our commitment to fostering holistic development and nurturing talent across various domains, we are proud to introduce CricKingdom Cricket Academy by Rohit Sharma. CricKingdom Cricket Academy stands as a premier destination for aspiring cricketers, offering world-class training facilities, expert cricket coaching, and comprehensive development programs. Under the esteemed guidance of cricketing legend Rohit Sharma, the academy aims to cultivate a culture of excellence, sportsmanship, and perseverance among young athletes. Our state-of-the-art infrastructure includes well-maintained cricket pitches, modern cricket training equipment, and personalized coaching sessions designed to cater to individuals of all skill levels. Beyond cricketing skills, our holistic approach emphasizes character building, teamwork, and leadership development, ensuring that athletes emerge as well-rounded individuals prepared for success both on and off the field.

Bhaichung Bhutia Football School

“The ball is round, the game lasts ninety minutes, and everything else is just theory.” – Josef Herberger The football ground at Ramagya School is all set with the finest of all Astro Turfs available across the globe. Spread over a huge area of lush green serenity, the new AstroTurf at Ramagya School football ground is suited to complement the needs of the players, making play conditions amicable during every season. Keeping in view the amenities provided at Ramagya School, the Bhaichung Bhutia Football School has stepped up to coach the football enthusiasts at Ramagya School to levels ahead of the beginning. The Bhaichung Bhutia Football School has a glorious history to produce exceptionally great football players for the country. Bhaichung Bhutia Football Academy is a 4-star rated academy by the AIFF (All India Football Federation) under the academy accreditation program and participates in all (U13, U15 & U18) AIFF Youth Leagues and is the only 4 stars rated academy in north India. Continuing the flagship of quality sportsmanship, Ramagya School has empanelled with the BBFS to coach the junior and senior kids in the ratio of 1 coach per 10 kids only, thus making no compromise on the quality of football skill which any child could imbibe.

NEGI

Martial Arts are aimed to increase cognitive skills, sharpen hand and eye coordination, and improve an individual’s sense of balance and direction. A sharp mind that grasps quickly and a skilled fighter who reacts quickly to physical threats is an unparalleled combination. At Ramagya School, we offer top-notch training in Mixed Martial Arts which is managed by Negi’s School of Martial Arts. Negi’s School of Martial Arts has over 2 decades of training experience. Under Martial Arts, kids, and adults can learn various disciplines like Kick-boxing; Tae-Kwon-do; Street fight; Karate; Kung fu; Muay Thai. The training is given under the supervision of great masters who have successfully organized the ‘Mission Fight back’ Martial Arts campaign and have also been awarded by the Government of India for proving their mettle in Martial Arts.

Wellflow

Welcome to the world of Pilates, where every movement brings you closer to a healthier, happier you! Ramagya with Wellflow Pilates & Yoga Studio, brings the transformative benefits of Pilates to our students and faculty. Pilates, developed by Joseph Pilates in the early 20th century, has emerged as a popular form of exercise, renowned for its ability to enhance overall body awareness. Through this partnership, Ramagya School aims to provide students with access to specialized Pilates programs tailored to their unique needs and goals. Led by certified instructors from Wellflow Pilates Studio, these programs will focus on core strength, flexibility, balance, and body awareness, fostering physical fitness and mental well-being. Whether you're new to Pilates or looking to deepen your practice, our pilate classes offer a welcoming and supportive environment for individuals of all ages and fitness levels. With state-of-the-art equipment and personalized instruction, students can expect to improve their functional movement patterns, enhance athletic performance, and reduce the risk of injury. Let’s embark on a journey to fitness and well-being through the power of Pilates classes in noida. Experience the transformative benefits of this holistic practice and unlock your full potential both on and off the mat!

TABLE-TENNIS

The popularity of Table Tennis has increased by leaps & bounds over the years. Like most sports, table tennis offers great mind-body stimulation, aerobic exercise, and social interaction. Some of the many health benefits of table tennis include: Improving hand-eye coordination. An intense game of table tennis stimulates mental alertness and concentration and develops mental acuity. In association with the ‘Alight table-tennis excellence centre’, the school offers world-class training for students with no prior experience to national medal-winning students. The school has strong infrastructure facility with 8 internationally sized tables and guidance from the national level coaches.

DANCEWORX

To create a higher awareness & understanding of international dance, Ramagya School has collaborated with the DanceWorx by the artistic dance director – Ashley Lobo, which facilitates evolving artists with top-notch dance classes in Noida. The objective of the dance classes is to discover the talent in them and to nurture it and take it to the highest possible level. The programme also emphasizes boosting self-confidence and helping individuals getting in touch with their emotions, and expressing them through dance moves and expressions. Students are also taught that dance is not limited to steps alone, but includes a wider understanding of self, creativity, movement, and physiology. This knowledge enables them to create their own work and eventually discover and develop a movement style of their own.
