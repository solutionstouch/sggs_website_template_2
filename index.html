SRI RAMOTSAV AYODHYA CONCLAVE: A CULTURAL TAPESTRY UNVEILED AT RAMAGYA SCHOOL, NOIDA

In a cultural journey that captivated attendees, the Delhi chapter of the “Sri Ramotsav Ayodhya” Conclave at Ramagya School, Noida, showcased a spellbinding tapestry of traditions. The event was graced by the esteemed presence of Hon’ble Minister Smriti Zubin Irani. The sacred rituals of Ram Paduka Pujan seamlessly fused time-honored traditions with a touch of modernity, creating a harmonious blend. Minister Irani shared profound insights into the seamless transmission of Indian heritage to the younger generation.
Distinguished luminaries present included Chief Guest Ms. Smriti Zubin Irani (Minister of Women and Child Development), Ms. Rina Singh (Principal of Ramagya School, Sector 50), Ms. Rajni Gupta (Director of Ramagya Group), Mr. Utkarsh Gupta (Managing Director – Ramagya Group of Schools), and Dr. Sanjay Gupta (Chairman, Ramagya Group), enhancing the prestige of the event. It transformed into a jubilant celebration of devotion and empowerment, where spirituality seamlessly intertwined with contemporary relevance!
