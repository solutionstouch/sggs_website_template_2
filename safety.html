The school has 300 cameras to monitor everything happening at school and to prevent the mishaps far before they happen.

Ramagya understands that today, the greatest risk that students face is on the road. The school takes active measures in keeping the students safe while they move to and fro from school.

  • We take effective steps of training the bus drivers to prevent unsafe behaviours and
  • we have also appointed maids who guard the students during the whole travelling time and minimize the risk of students crossing the road (if required) by accompanying them.
  • Also, all the Ramagyan buses have GPS, which makes it easier for the parents to track down the school bus at any point in time.

Keeping schools safe allows children to look forward to being in an encouraging environment that promotes social and creative learning. When their basic safety needs arenâ€™t met, children are at risk for not feeling comfortable at school and may stop showing up, or they may remain on edge throughout the day.

Ramagya understands that promoting school safety creates an open space for kids to explore, learn and grow. The school yields special care of the security of the students at school and hence has a collaboration with the best security company that has strict policies on school safety, promote increased learning, feelings of school unity, higher levels of pro-social behaviour and decreased levels of violence.
